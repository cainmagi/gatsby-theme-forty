In this example, we show an example of using external JS:

```yaml
custom_css:
  - https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/semantic-ui/2.4.1/components/input.min.css
  - https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/semantic-ui/2.4.1/components/label.min.css
custom_js:
  - https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/semantic-ui/2.4.1/semantic.min.js
  - https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/jquery/3.6.0/jquery.min.js
```

The following results are rendered by the external lib: `semantic-ui`.

<div class='ui input'>
  <input id='example'/>
</div>
